## What is Marketing?
Marketing in the digital era is no longer just about selling products; it's about **predicting consumer needs** through data analysis and creating successful value propositions.

## The Marketing Mix (4Ps)
The fundamental pillars of any marketing strategy:
- **Product**: The solution to a consumer problem.
- **Price**: The value exchanged, influenced by market positioning.
- **Place (Punto Vendita)**: Where the product meets the customer.
- **Promotion**: How the value is communicated.

## SWOT Analysis
A strategic planning technique used to identify:
- **Strengths** & **Weaknesses** (Internal factors)
- **Opportunities** & **Threats** (External factors)
